# Disclaimer

**Pharmacographer is educational software. It is not a medical device, and
nothing it produces is medical advice.**

- **Educational use only.** The purpose of this tool is to teach pharmacokinetic
  (PK) concepts — how concentration-vs-time curves arise from a model and its
  assumptions. It is for learning, not for treating anyone.
- **Not for clinical use.** Do not use it to make any decision about a real
  patient, including (but not limited to) choosing a drug, a dose, a route, or a
  dosing schedule. It deliberately does **not** take individual patient data and
  return a dose or clinical recommendation, and it never will.
- **Illustrative, not predictive.** Every curve is the output of a simplified
  mathematical **model** (v1: one-compartment, linear PK) applied to literature
  parameters for an **illustrative reference subject** (e.g. 70 kg). Real people
  differ enormously due to age, weight, organ function, genetics, formulation,
  food, drug interactions, and disease. A curve here is not a prediction for any
  individual.
- **Data may be inaccurate or incomplete.** Parameters are curated from public
  sources (e.g. FDA labels) and are subject to curation error, source
  disagreement, and simplification. Many values are **derived** or **inferred**,
  not directly measured — the app flags these, but the data carries no guarantee
  of accuracy or fitness for any purpose.
